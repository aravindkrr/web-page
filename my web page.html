Going “Green” means taking measures to become environmentally conscious in your decisions – using various every day methods to reduce the harm that your living does to the environment.
Here are some 5 simple and basic tips for how to make a green and clean Earth
Save electricity. If we save electricity you can save our earth's energy as well as your pocket because every single time you use electricity you pay for it so use electricity wisely. Use public transport. Take a walk or use bicycles. Save water.
